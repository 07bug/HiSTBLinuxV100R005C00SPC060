Purpose: to demonstrate how to communicate to smart card with calling function of SCI module .

Command: 
	./sample_sci    communication without PPS  
	./sample_complex_sci     communication with PPS
        
Parameter: input protocol type, output type of clock pin,etc,followed commond

Notice: to insure that the hardward connection between smart card slot and chipset is correctly
        and that the other enable switches are enabled.
